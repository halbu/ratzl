r a t z l
rätsel (fig: = geheimnis) riddle, mystery, enigma
how to play
swipe letter tiles to swap them with their neighbour. creating a word 3 letters or longer removes its tiles from the board. new tiles are fed in from the top of the board. below the game board you can see which tiles are coming next.
scoring
each word scores points (3 letters: 25pts, 4 letters: 100pts, 5 letters: 500pts). these points are multiplied by your multiplier and added to your score. every word you create increases your multiplier by 1. if you make a move that does not create a word, your multiplier is halved.
miscellanea
ratzl is deterministic. there is one challenge per day, and for each challenge, the board and letters are always the same for everybody.
